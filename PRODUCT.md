# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Casual groups of friends playing short online sessions together. Players need to
read the table quickly, understand the current risk, and enjoy each reveal
without learning a dense strategy-game interface.

## Product Purpose

Create an approachable online multiplayer push-your-luck game inspired by Flip
Seven, reimagined through explicit figures from Greek mythology. Success means a
group can enter a room, understand the board at a glance, and share dramatic,
memorable card reveals with minimal friction.

## Brand Personality

Mythic, expressive, and approachable. The world can move between serious,
battle-ready intensity and lighter character moments according to the figure,
while the product remains welcoming to casual players.

## Anti-references

- Not a solemn museum treatment of classical Greece.
- Not uniformly cute, chibi, or comedic.
- Not a dense collectible-card-game interface with rules competing against art.
- Not an imitation of the supplied Favour of Olympus reference artwork.
- Not reliant on color alone to communicate card identity or game state.

## Design Principles

- Gameplay reads before mythology: values and effects remain clear at a glance.
- Character drives expression: each figure can carry a distinct mood and energy.
- Restraint creates hierarchy: number cards stay quieter so god power-ups feel
  consequential.
- Reveals carry the spectacle: save the strongest visual and motion treatments
  for meaningful game events.
- The table stays social: controls and status support the group without crowding
  the shared play space.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for product UI. Keep card identity legible without color, provide
meaningful text alternatives for character artwork, preserve keyboard access,
and respect reduced-motion preferences.
